Which artists appeared on the 7th November 1980 show?
TV
★ Tommy Vance
Ah, great question! On that Friday night — 7th November 1980 — I spun tracks from Saxon, Hawkwind, Hibiscus, Rush, Ozzy Osbourne, Thin Lizzy, Scorpions, Jon Anderson, and the Eagles. We also had three superb sessions: Hibiscus recording fresh from the studio, and two archive sessions from John Mayall & The Bluesbreakers.
